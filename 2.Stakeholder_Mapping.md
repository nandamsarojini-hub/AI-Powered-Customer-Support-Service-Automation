# Stakeholder Mapping

This document identifies the key individuals and groups involved in, or impacted by, the AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation project.

## 1. Project Sponsor
* **Role:** Executive leadership or academic mentor.
* **Responsibilities:** Defines overarching business objectives, reviews system capabilities, and provides final approval on project deliverables.
* **Impact Level:** High

## 2. Salesforce System Administrator
* **Role:** The core developer and platform manager.
* **Responsibilities:** Configures custom objects, implements Prompt Builder templates, sets up Agentforce automated features, and manages all backend security and sharing settings.

## 3. Inspection Team (Primary End-Users)
* **Role:** Field workers or technical inspectors.
* **Responsibilities:** Creates and updates inspection records, utilizing the AI-generated summaries to translate technical findings into readable business context.

## 4. Support Team
* **Role:** Customer-facing service agents.
* **Responsibilities:** Relies on the automated system to quickly review AI-summarized inspection data, enabling them to provide accurate, timely, and informed assistance to customers.

## 5. Management & Operations
* **Role:** Strategic decision-makers.
* **Responsibilities:** Utilizes Salesforce Reports and Dashboards to gain visibility into inspection volumes, AI deflection rates, and SLA compliance.

## 6. IT Support
* **Role:** System maintenance personnel.
* **Responsibilities:** Ensures ongoing platform stability, monitors flow error logs, and resolves technical issues to maintain uninterrupted operations.
