# Stakeholder Mapping

Stakeholder mapping identifies the individuals and groups involved in or impacted by the AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation project. 

## 1. Project Sponsor
* **Role:** Defines business objectives and provides final approval on deliverables.
* **Impact:** High. Ensures the project aligns with overall business goals.

## 2. Salesforce System Administrator
* **Role:** Responsible for configuring the core system.
* **Responsibilities:** Implementing Prompt Builder, setting up Agentforce features, and managing security settings and permissions.

## 3. Inspection Team (Primary End-Users)
* **Role:** The on-the-ground users of the application.
* **Responsibilities:** Creating and managing inspection records, and utilizing AI-generated summaries to drastically improve their daily workflow efficiency.

## 4. Support Team
* **Role:** Customer-facing representatives.
* **Responsibilities:** Relies on the automated system to review inspection information quickly and provide accurate, timely customer assistance when required.

## 5. Management Stakeholders
* **Role:** Strategic decision-makers.
* **Responsibilities:** Uses the system's analytics and dashboards to gain visibility into inspection activities and outcomes for data-driven decision-making.

## 6. IT Support
* **Role:** Technical maintenance.
* **Responsibilities:** Ensures ongoing system stability and resolves any technical issues to maintain smooth operations.
