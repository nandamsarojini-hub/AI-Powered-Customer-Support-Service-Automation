# Maintenance, Monitoring & Troubleshooting

## Overview
Maintenance, Monitoring, and Troubleshooting ensures the AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation system continues to operate smoothly, securely, and efficiently after deployment. This phase focuses on maintaining system stability while supporting ongoing business operations.

---

## 1. Maintenance Activities
Regular maintenance is conducted to ensure the system adapts correctly as case volumes and business requirements evolve.
* **Configuration Reviews:** Periodic evaluations of automation flows, validation rules, support queues, and Agentforce configurations to ensure sustained accuracy.
* **Platform Updates:** Salesforce platform release updates are actively evaluated and tested to maintain compatibility and optimize system performance.

## 2. System Monitoring
Proactive monitoring is performed to identify performance issues or failures within Salesforce before they impact the end-user experience.
* **Metrics Tracked:** Ongoing tracking of case assignment accuracy, flow execution limits, AI response effectiveness, and overall user activity.
* **Tools Utilized:** System logs, flow error emails, and custom Salesforce reports are used to detect and alert administrators of potential system failures.

## 3. Troubleshooting & Issue Resolution
A structured protocol is followed to quickly diagnose and resolve system anomalies.
* **Common Issues Addressed:** Rapid resolution of failed automations, incorrect case routing, AI response mismatches, or permission-related access errors.
* **Resolution Workflow:** 
  1. Conduct a Root Cause Analysis (RCA) on the reported issue.
  2. Develop and apply fixes within a secure Salesforce Sandbox environment.
  3. Validate the solution through testing.
  4. Safely deploy the finalized fix to the Production environment.
