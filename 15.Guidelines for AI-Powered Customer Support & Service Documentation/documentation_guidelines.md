# Guidelines for AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Documentation

## Overview
The documentation submission for the AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation project should provide a clear and detailed explanation of the system's objectives, functional scope, and overall architecture. 

## Key Requirements
*   **Process Automation:** The documentation must describe exactly how customer support processes are automated utilizing Salesforce features alongside AI-driven capabilities.
*   **Technical Specifications:** It is required to include comprehensive information on the following components implemented on Salesforce:
    *   Data models
    *   Object relationships
    *   Validation rules
    *   Automation flows
    *   Security configurations
    *   AI components (Prompt Builder, Agentforce)

## Formatting Standards
To ensure the system is easy to understand, evaluate, and maintain in the future, all content must follow a highly structured format. This includes:
*   Proper hierarchical headings.
*   Relevant architectural and flow diagrams.
*   Clear, concise, and professional explanations of all technical implementations.
