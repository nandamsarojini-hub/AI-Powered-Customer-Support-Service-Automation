# Guidelines for AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation Salesforce Project Demo Video

## AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation – Demo Steps

*   **Introduction** – Introduce yourself, project title, and purpose of automating customer support using Salesforce.
*   **App Overview** – Show the custom app from App Launcher and explain key objects used.
*   **UI Demonstration** – Demonstrate record creation, validation rules, and page navigation.
*   **Automation Showcase** – Show flows, **3-Day Reminder Flow**, approval process, and AI assistance.
*   **Security Setup** – Explain profiles, roles, permission sets, queues, and sharing rules.
*   **Reports & Dashboards** – Display reports and dashboards for case insights.
*   **Debugging & Fixes** – Briefly explain how issues were identified and resolved.
*   **Conclusion** – Summarize project outcomes and key learnings.
